About Matt Mills

Matt Mills is a North Texas reining professional based in Millsap, known for producing and showing horses with consistent performance in the open division. A skilled trainer and competitor with a strong foundation in NRHA competition.

Career Highlights

General Overview
  • ◆NRHA open division competitor with multiple major event credits

Discipline Specialization

Mills competes with a style focused on consistent pattern execution — steady scoring that accumulates well across multiple runs. His horses are trained to perform reliably under pressure, a key trait for fantasy selections in formats that reward consistency.
