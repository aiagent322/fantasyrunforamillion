The Format

How Team Selection Works

Fantasy Run For A Million uses a simple draft format. Pick the riders you believe will perform — then let the results score your team.

01

Select riders from each discipline — reining, cow horse, and cutting — to fill your team roster.

02

Follow Results

03

Earn Points

Top finishes earn the most points. Every qualified run earns something. See the full scoring rules →

04

Compete on the Leaderboard
